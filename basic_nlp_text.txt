Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a field of Artificial Intelligence.
It focuses on enabling computers to understand, interpret, and generate
human language.

Sample sentences: 1. I love learning NLP. 2. Sentiment analysis helps
identify positive and negative opinions. 3. Tokenization splits text
into smaller units called tokens. 4. Vectorization converts text into
numerical representations. 5. Machine learning models can analyze large
amounts of text data.
